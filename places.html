Favourite Spots

Beijing, China - Great Wall of China, Forbidden City, Temple of Heaven:

Beijing is a city of history and culture, with some of the world's most iconic landmarks. The Great Wall of China is an engineering marvel and is one of the seven wonders of the world. The Forbidden City is a UNESCO World Heritage site and was once the imperial palace of the Ming and Qing Dynasties. The Temple of Heaven is another attraction, showcasing the ancient Chinese religion and architecture.

Dubai, UAE - Burj Khalifa, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Mall:

Dubai is a city of dreams and a favorite destination for many tourists. The city is full of life and excitement with world-renowned landmarks like the Burj Khalifa, Palm Jumeirah, and Dubai Mall. Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world, Palm Jumeirah is a man-made island in the shape of a palm tree, and Dubai Mall is one of the largest shopping malls globally, making Dubai a perfect mix of modernity and tradition.

Paris, France - Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral:

Paris is the city of love, art, and culture, making it a favorite spot for many travelers. The Eiffel Tower is the iconic landmark of Paris and is an engineering masterpiece. The Louvre Museum is the world's largest art museum and houses some of the world's most famous masterpieces like the Mona Lisa. Notre-Dame Cathedral is another attraction, known for its Gothic architecture and historical significance.

Madina - Masjid e Nabvi:

Madina is the city in Saudi Arabia that is very close to my heart because of the Prophet's Mosque or Masjid e Nabvi. This is the second holiest site in Islam after the Khana Kaaba and the burial place of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). The city is full of spiritual and historical significance, and visiting it is a humbling and rewarding experience.

Makkah - Khana Kaaba:

Makkah is one of my favorite spots to visit one day because it holds the spiritual center of the Muslim world, the Khana Kaaba. This is the holiest site in Islam and the destination of the Hajj pilgrimage, a once-in-a-lifetime experience for every Muslim. Visiting Makkah and praying at the Khana Kaaba is a dream for every Muslim, and I hope to fulfill this dream one day.

Santorini, Greece - Oia, Akrotiri, Red Beach:

Santorini is a beautiful island in Greece with breathtaking views and landscapes. The picturesque village of Oia is the most popular spot with its stunning blue-domed churches and whitewashed houses. The ancient city of Akrotiri is another attraction, showcasing the ruins of an ancient Minoan city. Lastly, the red beach with its distinctive red and black volcanic pebbles and clear blue waters is a must-visit.
